### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**SaaS Professional** (€299/month):
- Average lifetime: 36 months
- LTV: €299 × 36 = **€10,764**
- Churn rate: 5% annually

**On-Premise Enterprise** (€75K + €10K/year):
- Initial: €75K
- Support (3 years): €30K
- LTV: **€105K**

**API Integration** (€500/month + usage):
- Average lifetime: 48 months
- Monthly revenue: €500 + €200 usage = €700
- LTV: €700 × 48 = **€33,600**

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**SaaS**: €500 per customer
- Marketing: €200
- Sales effort: €300

**On-Premise**: €5,000 per license
- Sales cycle: 3-6 months
- Direct sales effort

**API**: €10,000 per integration
- Technical integration support
- Long-term partnership

**LTV/CAC Ratios**:
- SaaS: 21.5x (excellent)
- On-Premise: 21x (excellent)
- API: 3.4x (good)
