- **Win Rate:** Target > 20%
- **Sales Cycle:** Target < 60 days
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Target < EUR 5k
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** Target > 20:1

- **NPS Score:** Target > 40
- **Customer Retention:** Target > 90%
- **Expansion ARR:** Target > 20% of new ARR
