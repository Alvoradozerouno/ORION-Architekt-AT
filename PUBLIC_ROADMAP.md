## 📅 How Roadmap Works

### Commitment Levels
- **✅ Committed:** Funded, started, or nearly complete
- **🔄 In Progress:** Active development, on track
- **📅 Planned:** Estimated timeline, subject to change
- **💡 Exploring:** Under research, may pivot

### Flexibility
- Roadmap changes based on customer feedback
- Market changes (economy, regulations) may shift priorities
- Major bug fixes take priority over features
- Community voting can influence timeline

### Transparency
- Updates published monthly
- Delays communicated in advance
- Rationale shared when items are re-prioritized
