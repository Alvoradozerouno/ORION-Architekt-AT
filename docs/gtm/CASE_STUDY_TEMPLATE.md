## CASE STUDY WRITING TIPS

### Do's ✅
- Use specific numbers (not "significant savings")
- Include direct customer quotes
- Focus on business impact, not features
- Tell a story (situation → challenge → solution → result)
- Get customer approval before publishing
- Include permission to use name/company logo

### Don'ts ❌
- Exaggerate or round up unrealistically
- Make claims customer hasn't verified
- Focus too much on your product features
- Publish without customer consent
- Include confidential information
- Use jargon customers didn't use

# CASE STUDY: Architektur Wien - EUR 50k Annual Savings in 6 Months

## Quick Stats
- **Company:** Architektur Wien GmbH, Wien, Austria
- **Size:** 18 employees
- **Industry:** Architecture (residential + commercial)
- **Challenge:** Multi-Bundesland compliance bottleneck, high licensing costs
- **Result:** EUR 50k annual savings + 60% faster compliance checks
- **ROI:** Payback in 11 months

## The Challenge

### Company Background
Architektur Wien is a 18-person firm specializing in residential projects across Austria. They typically handle 20-25 projects per year across Wien, Niederösterreich, and Salzburg, working with developers and private clients.

### Problems They Faced

1. **Problem #1: Multi-Bundesland Complexity**
   - Evidence: Each Bundesland required different software setups
   - Impact: 3-4 hours per project on rule verification alone

2. **Problem #2: High Licensing Costs**
   - Evidence: EUR 75k/year (Revit × 12 users + compliance tools)
   - Impact: 23% of their IT budget going to software

3. **Problem #3: Compliance Uncertainty**
   - Evidence: 1-2 compliance rejections per year (~8% error rate)
   - Impact: EUR 4-6k rework per rejection

### Why They Sought a Solution

**Martin, Principal Architect:** "We were sending projects to magistrates and 8% came back for compliance issues. That's 2 projects a year that needed rework. At EUR 3-5k per rework, that's real money."

---

## The Solution: ORION

### Why They Chose ORION
They evaluated Revit (too expensive), Allplan (Germany-focused), and stayed with their current stack. Then discovered ORION's unique 9-Bundesland support. Martin said: "For the first time, I could handle Wien, NÖ, and Salzburg rules in ONE system."

### How They Used ORION
- **Primary use case:** OIB-RL compliance verification before submission
- **Secondary use case:** Material selection optimization
- **Key features:** Compliance engine, multi-Bundesland dashboard, audit trails
- **Integration:** DWG export to their existing CAD workflow

### Implementation
- **Timeline:** 2 weeks to full team productivity (8 people)
- **Training:** 4 hours initial training + 2 hours follow-up
- **Challenges:** Staff initially skeptical ("Do we really need another tool?") but quickly adopted after seeing first project saved 5 hours

---

## The Results

### Quantified Impact

#### Time Savings
- **Before:** 6 hours per project for compliance verification
- **After:** 0.5 hours (mostly automation)
- **Annual Savings:** 5.5 hours × 25 projects × EUR 80/hour = EUR 11,000

#### Cost Reduction
- **Before:** EUR 75,000/year (Revit + tools)
- **After:** EUR 45,000/year (ORION)
- **Annual Savings:** EUR 30,000

#### Error Reduction
- **Before:** 2 compliance rejections/year (~8% rate)
- **After:** 0 rejections in first 12 months
- **Benefit:** EUR 8,000/year (avoided rework)

#### Capacity Increase
- **Before:** 25 projects/year at capacity
- **After:** 30 projects/year (6% revenue increase)
- **Additional Revenue:** 5 projects × EUR 50k avg = EUR 250,000

### Total 12-Month Impact
| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Time Saved** | EUR 11,000 | Compliance checking automation |
| **Cost Reduced** | EUR 30,000 | Licensing reduction |
| **Risk Reduced** | EUR 8,000 | Fewer compliance errors |
| **Revenue Gained** | EUR 250,000 | 6% additional capacity |
| **TOTAL BENEFIT** | **EUR 299,000** | **Massive value** |
| **ORION Annual Cost** | EUR 45,000 | 18-person firm tier |
| **NET BENEFIT** | **EUR 254,000** | **ROI: 565%!** |

### Customer Testimonial

**Martin Hofmann, Principal Architect at Architektur Wien:**

> "ORION changed how we work. Before, I had to manually verify each project's compliance for Wien, NÖ, and Salzburg. Now? Automatic. Zero compliance rejections in a year. And we're saving EUR 30k on software alone. Best business decision we made."

## Key Insights

### What Worked Well
1. **Rapid Adoption:** Team saw immediate value in first project
2. **Multi-BL Support:** Their #1 pain point solved immediately
3. **Cost Savings:** Replaced 4 different tools with one system

### Lessons Learned
1. **Staff Buy-In Critical:** Initial skepticism disappeared after first project
2. **Training Matters:** 4 hours initial training prevented adoption issues
3. **Migration is Easy:** DWG import worked better than expected

### Advice for Similar Firms
"If you're working across multiple Bundesländer, ORION is no-brainer. The compliance automation alone pays for itself in 3 months. Add the cost savings and multi-Bundesland support, and it's a no-brainer."
