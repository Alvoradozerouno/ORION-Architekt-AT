# 👥 ORION CUSTOMER PROFILES & IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)

## Target Customer Personas

### Persona 1: "Martin" - Principal Architect (Mid-Market)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 45-55, Principal/CEO of 15-person firm
- Location: Wien, Salzburg, or other major city
- Experience: 20+ years in architecture
- Tech-Savvy: Medium

**Pain Points:**
- EUR 45k/year spent on software licenses (Revit + plugins)
- Manual compliance verification is error-prone
- Managing projects across multiple Bundesländer is complex
- Compliance audits take 2-3 days per project

**What Matters to Martin:**
1. **ROI (most important)** - Wants EUR 40k+ savings documented
2. **Compliance certainty** - Doesn't want compliance rejections
3. **Team adoption** - Needs software that "just works"
4. **Support** - Wants responsive support when issues arise

**How to Sell to Martin:**
- Lead with ROI: "EUR 35-50k annual savings"
- Show compliance automation
- Provide 2-3 reference customers (similar size/location)
- Emphasize ease of adoption
- Offer 30-day trial with money-back guarantee

**Timeline:** 30-45 days from first contact to decision

**Timeline:** 45-60 days from first contact to decision

## ICP Summary

### The "Perfect" ORION Customer

**Company Profile:**
- Size: 10-50 employees
- Sector: Architecture, structural engineering, or BIM consulting
- Geography: Projects across multiple Austrian Bundesländer (2+)
- Annual SW Budget: EUR 30-100k
- Growth Stage: Established, profitable, looking to scale

**Behavioral Profile:**
- Decision-making: 30-45 days from first contact to close
- Evaluation Process: Wants to try before buying (trial)
- Reference-sensitive: Wants to talk to similar firms
- Risk-averse: Wants guarantees (money-back, SLA)
- Pragmatic: Focuses on ROI, not features

**Problem Profile:**
- Current pain: Multiple tools, high compliance risk, cost
- Motivation: EUR 40k+ annual savings + compliance certainty
- Urgency: Medium (not emergency, but solving soon)
- Budget: Approved/available

**Success Profile (after 90 days):**
- Usage: > 80% of team adopts platform
- Savings: EUR 10-15k documented in first 90 days
- Satisfaction: NPS > 40 (would recommend)
- Advocacy: Ready to be reference customer

## Segmentation Strategy

### Tier 1: "High-Touch Enterprise" (20-30% of revenue target)
- 50+ person firms
- EUR 150-300k/year ARR per customer
- Sales approach: Direct, dedicated account manager
- CAC: EUR 8-15k (acceptable for larger deals)

### Tier 2: "Mid-Market" (50-60% of revenue target)
- 10-50 person firms
- EUR 30-80k/year ARR per customer
- Sales approach: Direct sales + self-serve trial
- CAC: EUR 3-5k (most efficient)

### Tier 3: "Emerging/Consultants" (10-20% of revenue target)
- 1-10 person firms or consultants
- EUR 5-30k/year ARR per customer
- Sales approach: Inbound + community marketing
- CAC: EUR 1-2k (very efficient)
