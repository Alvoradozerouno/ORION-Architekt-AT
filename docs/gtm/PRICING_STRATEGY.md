# 💳 PRICING STRATEGY & LICENSING MODELS

## Pricing Philosophy

ORION pricing is based on:
1. **Value-based pricing** (not cost-based)
2. **Simplicity** (one price per firm size, not per-user complexity)
3. **Accessibility** (affordable vs competitors while sustainable)
4. **Predictability** (fixed annual pricing, no surprises)

## PAYMENT MODELS

### Annual Subscription (Default)
- Payment: One payment upfront for full year
- Discount: No additional discount (this IS the discount)
- Term: Auto-renews unless cancelled

### Monthly Subscription
- Payment: Monthly billing
- Surcharge: +20% vs annual (annual EUR 45k = monthly EUR 975)
- Term: Month-to-month, cancel anytime
- Ideal for: Trial, evaluation

### Multi-Year Discount
- 2-Year Contract: 5% discount
- 3-Year Contract: 10% discount
- Example: EUR 45k × 3 years × 0.9 = EUR 121,500 (saves EUR 13,500)

## PRICING FAQ

**Q: Why no per-user pricing like Revit?**
> A: We found per-user pricing penalizes growth. Our model rewards scaling.

**Q: Can I get a discount for multi-year commitment?**
> A: Yes! 5% for 2 years, 10% for 3 years.

**Q: Do you have monthly billing?**
> A: Yes, +20% premium vs annual. Most customers prefer annual.

**Q: What if our team grows mid-year?**
> A: No problem. Annual price covers unlimited users, so growth is free.

**Q: Can I upgrade mid-contract?**
> A: Yes. We pro-rate the upgrade (no waste).

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
> A: 30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked.
