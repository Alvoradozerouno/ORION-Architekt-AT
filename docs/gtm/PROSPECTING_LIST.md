## TRACKING

### Status Codes
- 🟡 **PROSPECT:** Not yet contacted
- 🔵 **REACHED:** Initial contact made, waiting response
- 🟢 **INTERESTED:** Demo scheduled or call confirmed
- ⭐ **QUALIFIED:** Actively evaluating, strong fit
- 💰 **CLOSE PENDING:** In negotiation, likely to close
- ✅ **CUSTOMER:** Signed deal
- ❌ **LOST:** Passed on ORION

### Lead Scoring
Rate each prospect 1-10 on:
- **Fit** (0-10): How well does ORION solve their problems
- **Authority** (0-10): Is this person decision-maker?
- **Timeline** (0-10): When do they want to decide?
- **Budget** (0-10): Do they have budget?

**Total Score** = (Fit + Authority + Timeline + Budget) / 4
