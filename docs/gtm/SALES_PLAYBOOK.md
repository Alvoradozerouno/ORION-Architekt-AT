## SECTION 2: SALES METHODOLOGY

### 2.1 The SOLID Sales Process

- S — SITUATION (Initial Outreach)
- O — OPPORTUNITY (Discovery Call)
- L — LOSS (Problem Deep-Dive)
- I — IMPLEMENTATION (Solution Fit Demo)
- D — DECISION (Close)

### 2.2 Sales Tactics by Stage

**SITUATION:** Cold outreach via LinkedIn, email, Kammer events
**OPPORTUNITY:** 20-minute discovery call to qualify
**LOSS:** Deep dive into their specific pain points
**IMPLEMENTATION:** Live demo using their use case
**DECISION:** Assumptive close or trial offer

### 2.3 Handling Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "Saves EUR 35-50k annually. ROI in 12 months." |
| "Need to evaluate" | "30-day free trial, money-back guarantee" |
| "Competitors are cheaper" | "They don't support all 9 Bundesländer natively." |
| "Our IT needs approval" | "Send me requirements, I'll prepare assessment" |
